## About Me

I am Adigun Micheal Damilare, a creative and analytical Digital Marketer, Social Media Manager, and Visual Designer based in Agege, Lagos. With over 5 years of hands-on experience, I've partnered with startups, SMEs, NGOs, and multinational clients to build powerful digital identities through storytelling, data, and design.

As a Mass Communication graduate and certified professional in Social Media Management, Data Analysis, and Web Design, I merge strategy with creativity to deliver measurable results—higher engagement, stronger brand recall, more leads, and increased revenue.

> "My work is guided by one principle: marketing that looks beautiful should also perform brilliantly."
